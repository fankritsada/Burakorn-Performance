# Risks

## Public launch risk

The site is built public-ready but defaults to noindex. Public indexing, production deployment, DNS changes, or live domain attachment require owner approval.

## Vehicle claim risk

The project references Honda Accord G8 K24 as a base platform only. A supplied hero image may show real vehicle marks, but copy must not imply Honda affiliation, endorsement, manufacturer relationship, or official partnership.

## Sales claim risk

Do not add prices, deposits, public stock lists, checkout, purchase CTAs, availability claims, full warranty language, no-risk language, or like-new claims.

## Content risk

Real exterior photos, service documents, inspection details, legal framing, and contact channel still need owner-approved source material before public launch.
